Common Attacks (TryHackMe)

Social Engineering

Manipulation techniques. Example: Stuxnet (worm virus replicated via USB).

Phishing

  • General Phishing: Broad emails.
  • Spearphishing: Targets specific individual/group.
  • Whaling: Targets high-value individuals (CEO, CFO).

Malware & Ransomware

Malware: Software designed to perform malicious actions.

Ransomware: Encrypts data to hold it for ransom. Goal is to infect as many systems as possible.

Defensive Measures

  • MFA (Multi-Factor Authentication): Use TOTP/OTP.
  • TLS (Transport Layer Security): Encrypts traffic.
  • VPN & HTTPS: Encrypted connections.
  • Update Software: Replace End of Life (EOL) software like Windows 7.
