Introduction to Information Security

InfoSec is safeguarding information and systems from unauthorized access, use, disclosure, disruption, modification, or destruction.

The CIA Triad

C
Confidentiality
I
Integrity
A
Availability

Core Concepts

  • Risk: Potential for a malicious event (Threat + Vulnerability).
  • Threat: Potential cause of an incident (Hacker, Fire).
  • Vulnerability: Weakness in a system (Bug, Weak Password).

Key Teams

  • Blue Team: Internal defense.
  • Red Team: Simulates attacks.
  • Purple Team: Collaboration between Red and Blue.

InfoSec Domains

  • Network Security: Firewalls, VPNs, IDS/IPS.
  • App Security: Security by design, OWASP.
  • Cloud Security: Shared responsibility model.
  • Physical Security: Locks, guards, access points.
  • OpSec: Asset and threat identification.

Threats

DDoS, Ransomware, Social Engineering (Phishing), Insider Threats, APTs.
