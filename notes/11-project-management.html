Project Management 101

Speaker: Engr. Abdullah Galo Jr.

Application of knowledge, skills, and techniques to project activities to meet requirements.

Triple Constraints

A project manager must balance these three:

  1. Scope: What is the project trying to accomplish?
  2. Time: How long should it take?
  3. Cost: How much should it cost?

Project Lifecycle (5 Phases)

  1. Initiating: Identifying business needs, project charter, stakeholders.
  2. Planning: Goals, teams, schedules, budget, risk management.
  3. Executing: The "work" starts. Coordinating teams and resources.
  4. Monitoring & Controlling: Comparing plan vs. actual. Managing changes and issues.
  5. Closing: Final deliverables, post-implementation review, releasing resources.

Stakeholder Management

Identifying and engaging individuals impacted by the project.

  • Internal: Sponsors, Directors, Business Units.
  • Team: Technical, Business, Project teams.
  • External: Investors, Media, Government, Suppliers.

Strategy: Inform, Persuade, Align, Feedback, Motivate.
